About MSU-MCEST

MSU-MCEST is a globally recognized institution in education, science, and technology, committed to advancing instruction, research, innovations, peace and development. With its education programs consistently achieving a 100% passing rate in the Licensure Examination for Teachers (LET) and producing board topnotchers, we continue to aim for excellence. The campus envisions further expansion into the fields of science, technology, and innovation, responding proactively to the educational and developmental needs of our surrounding communities.

MSU-MCEST is a premier public institution located in Maigo, Lanao del Norte, Philippines. It specializes in industrial innovation and technical education, offering a range of programs designed to equip students with cutting-edge knowledge and practical skills. As a COPC-certified institution, it has been recognized for its commitment to quality education and operational excellence.
